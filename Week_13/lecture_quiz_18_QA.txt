A medical student wants to estimate the mean height of women. 
She measures 100 women's heights and finds a mean of 165.1 cm 
and a standard deviation of 8.9 cm. What is the 90% confidence 
interval for the mean women's height?

A: (163.6cm, 166.6cm)
B: (163.4cm, 166.8cm)
C: (164.8cm, 165.4cm)
D: (164.0cm, 166.2cm)

Answer: A
