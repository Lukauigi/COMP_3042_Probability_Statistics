A medical student wants to estimate the mean height of women. 
She measures 10 women's heights and finds a mean of 165.1 cm and a 
standard deviation of 8.9 cm. The heights appear to come from a 
population that is normally distributed. What is the 90% confidence
interval for the mean women's height?

A: (159.9cm, 170.3cm)
B: (160.5cm, 169.7cm)
C: (160.0cm, 170.2cm)
D: (158.7cm, 171.5cm)

Answer: A
