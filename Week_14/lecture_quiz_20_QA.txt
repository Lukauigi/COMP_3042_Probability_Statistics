A student suspects that the mean amount of time BCIT students spend 
on homework is more than 3h per day. He decides to do a formal 
hypothesis test by surveying 50 BCIT students and asking them how 
much time they spent on homework today to see if his suspicion is 
correct. What are the null and alternative hypotheses for this test?

Question 1 options:

A: H0: μ≤3; H1: μ>3

B: H0: μ>3; H1: μ≤3

C: H0: μ<3; H1: μ≥3

D: H0: μ≥3; H1: μ<3

Answer: A
